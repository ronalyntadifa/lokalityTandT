#SANJUAN

San Juan is perfect for travelers who don't mind roughing it out by the beach. You can either rent a tent that comes with pillows, insulation foam, toiletries and free breakfast the next day, or just pitch your own gears.


San Juan is bounded on the north by the municipality of Bacnotan along the Baroro River, and on the east by the municipalities of San Gabriel and Bagulin along the Dasay-Duplas-Nagyubuyuban Creek.
