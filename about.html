MORE ABOUT LOKALITY

LOKALITY Travel and Tours started in November 2, 2018 as a private operating company whose head office is in City of San Fernando, La Union. It is an organization that aims to promote local communities that will also help in the tourism of the region. it aims to persuade people to explore their localities first before visiting other places. the group tries to show the beautiful places in a particular region by simply giving the locals and also the tourists the best and most affordable promos with the best accomodations.

VISION AND MISSION

Lokality Travel and Tours mission is to promote the local culture and to expose the undiscoered beuty of places. It aims to make people aware on the beauty of the country piece by piece. It envisions a country with people who knows more about their locality and to influence people to become a patriot of their own roots. Lokality Travel an Tours provide not only the best deals but also feeds people with new learnings, experiences and unforgettable adventures through the best and knowledgeable tour guides.
